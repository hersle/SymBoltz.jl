Predicting luminosity distances

To predict luminosity distances

\[d_L = \frac{r}{a} = \chi \, \mathrm{sinc} (\sqrt{k} \chi), \qquad \text{where} \qquad \chi = c \, (t_0 - t)\]

theoretically, we solve the standard ╬øCDM model:
